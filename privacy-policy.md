# Privacy Policy for AI NutriSnap

Effective date: April 26, 2026

AI NutriSnap ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share information when you use the AI NutriSnap mobile application.

## Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

### Account Information
- Name
- Email address
- Profile photo
- User ID

### User Content
- Meal photos you upload
- Nutrition results generated from your meal photos
- AI-generated health report content

### Purchase and Subscription Information
- Subscription status
- Purchased product identifier
- Renewal and expiration information

### Usage and Device Information
- Device identifiers
- App usage information
- Advertising-related data
- Basic technical and diagnostic information from third-party services

## How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

- Create and manage your account
- Authenticate sign-in
- Analyze meal photos and estimate nutrition
- Generate AI-powered nutrition reports
- Save your nutrition history
- Manage subscriptions and premium access
- Show ads for free-tier users
- Improve app functionality, reliability, and performance

## Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may collect or process data, including:

- Firebase Authentication
- Cloud Firestore
- Firebase Storage
- Google Sign-In
- Sign in with Apple
- RevenueCat
- Google AdMob
- Backend AI processing services

These services may process data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share data with third-party service providers only as needed to operate the app, including authentication, cloud storage, subscription management, advertising, and AI processing.

## Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as needed to provide the app’s functionality, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

If you deactivate or delete your account, we will process your data according to our retention and deletion practices.

## Your Choices

You may:

- Sign out of your account
- Request account deactivation
- Manage app permissions such as camera and photo library access
- Manage subscriptions through Apple App Store or Google Play

## Children’s Privacy

AI NutriSnap is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
